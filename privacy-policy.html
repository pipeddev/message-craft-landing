Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

MessageCraft respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Anonymous identifiers: We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to identify sessions without requiring personal user accounts.
  • User-provided content: Text entered by the user (such as message context or recipient) is sent to our backend service to generate messages.
  • Advertising data: We use Google AdMob, which may collect advertising identifiers to display ads.

2. How We Use Information

  • Generate messages using AI-based services
  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Display advertisements
  • Prevent abuse and ensure service stability

3. Third-Party Services

MessageCraft uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication)
  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Google Cloud Run (Backend infrastructure)

Each service processes data according to its own privacy policies.

4. Data Retention

MessageCraft does not permanently store generated messages or user inputs. Data is processed temporarily to generate responses and then discarded.

5. Children’s Information

MessageCraft is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Security

All data transmissions are encrypted in transit using secure communication protocols (HTTPS).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
